The Apple

Look! There is an apple.
It's a big, juicy apple.
Daddy, I want that apple.
Mmm... It's too high to reach.
I have a good idea.

Little more, daddy, little more.
I can't reach it! It's too high!
Stand on your tiptoes!
I have another idea.

I've almost got it, daddy.
You're very heavy!
Chip, be careful! Ok. Uh...

I've got it. Oh, no...
The apple is falling!

Catch it, mommy!
Oops, catch it, Biff!
Oop, catch it, Kipper!
Oh,no...
Woo...Woo...

Floppy is eating the apple!
Oh,no, Floppy...
It looks so yummy.
Ha.ha.ha, you're lucky, Floppy.
